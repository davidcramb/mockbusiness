Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the finest quality hats to the general public. From the basic trucker cap, to the silkiest tophat, we have a style that's right for you! Please take the time to browse our catalog and discover your style. Our expert phrenologists are on call 24/7 to assist.

Our History

Haberdashing, Inc. was founded in 1825 by Ludwig von Headinhat after discovering that powdered wigs caused a number of serious ailments, including impotence, lupis, incontinence, migraines, and cancer. He swore to bring the superior solution of the fitted hat to the general public. He found his greatest success late in life after fitting Abraham Lincoln for his top hat. The rest is history.
